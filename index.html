Exploring NYT's 5 Star Recipes

Although NYT Cooking allows users to explore their impressive variety of recipes with categorical filters, users are unable to filter published recipes by rating. I wanted to find NYT's best of the best, so I scraped NYT's published recipes with 5-star ratings (using R, most recently on Jun 26, 2023), then created this site to further explore these favorites.
